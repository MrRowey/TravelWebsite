Welcome to Terry Jones Travel

Terry Jones has worked in the travel industry for over 40 years. In 1992 he established Terry Jones Travel in Sandford Avenue, Church Stretton, Shropshire, which has been trading successfully as an independent Travel Agency ever since.


In 2013 Terry Jones Travel was pleased to open a new branch of the Travel Agency in Ludlow. Situated at the top of Castle Square, Ludlow, next to Ludlow College, here we also offer a personal , professional and totally independent travel service to the people of South Shropshire.
